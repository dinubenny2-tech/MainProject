Important Service Rules Customers Should Know

Please review the following service guidelines carefully to ensure smooth processing and better service experience.

Emergency Service Requests
Customers can mark a complaint as Emergency while submitting the request. Emergency services include extra charges, must have valid reasons, and are provided only after admin approval.
Replacement Policy
For replacement requests, customers must upload a clear bill photo and product photo. After verification, a mandatory replacement form will be sent to process the request with the company.
Worker Safety Policy
If any safety risks are present at the service location, our workers will not proceed with the job until a safe working environment is ensured.
Contact Number Issues
If the worker is unable to reach your registered phone number, the admin may update your contact details to ensure uninterrupted communication.
Site Visit Confirmation & Communication
The assigned worker will call the customer to confirm and fix the site visit date. For any other communication, both the worker and customer can use the platform’s chat system.
